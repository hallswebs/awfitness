PROGRAMME OVERVIEW

Fight Camp in a Programme

This isn't just boxing fitness — it's structured the same way Alfie prepares for his own professional fights. Advanced combinations, defensive skills, fight-pace conditioning, and the mental toughness to push through when it gets hard. Whether you want to compete or just train like someone who does.

Phase 1

Technical Upgrade

Weeks 1-3 — Sharpen fundamentals. Counter-punching, angles, pivots, advanced defensive movement. Build the engine.

Phase 2

Fight Conditioning

Weeks 4-7 — 3-minute rounds at fight pace. Combination chains, body work, pressure fighting and backing up. VO2 max work.

Phase 3

Peak & Perform

Weeks 8-10 — Simulated fight rounds, tapering, weight management. Arrive at peak condition, sharp and ready.
