PROGRAMME OVERVIEW

10 Weeks to Real Power

This programme uses Alfie's fighter-tested approach to kettlebell strength: heavy grinds, ballistic power, and conditioning that transfers to real-world performance. You'll train 4 days per week with a structured upper/lower and power/conditioning split.

Phase 1

Strength Base

Weeks 1-3 — Heavy doubles, single-arm work, and progressive loading. Build your strength ceiling.

Phase 2

Power Development

Weeks 4-7 — Cleans, snatches, and complexes. Develop explosive power and muscular endurance.

Phase 3

Peak Performance

Weeks 8-10 — Long-cycle complexes, density training, and test week. Achieve new PRs.
