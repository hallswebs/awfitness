PROGRAMME OVERVIEW

8 Weeks. Maximum Shred.

This programme is designed around the EPOC effect (excess post-exercise oxygen consumption) — meaning you keep burning calories for hours after each session. Sessions are deliberately shorter and more intense. Combined with the calorie-deficit nutrition plan, this is the fastest route to visible results.

Phase 1

Ignite

Weeks 1-3 — Introduce circuit training structure. Learn the movements, build work capacity, adjust to the deficit.

Phase 2

Accelerate

Weeks 4-6 — Increase intensity and density. Shorter rest, heavier bells, longer boxing rounds. This is where the fat melts.

Phase 3

Finish Strong

Weeks 7-8 — Peak intensity finishers, metabolic complexes, and a controlled refeed to show off your results.
