PROGRAMME OVERVIEW

12 Weeks to a Complete Transformation

This is the programme that Alfie wishes existed when he started training. It takes the best of everything — kettlebell strength, boxing conditioning, mobility, nutrition — and structures it into a progressive 12-week system that works for any level. Whether you're starting from scratch or you've been training for years, this programme meets you where you are and takes you further than you thought possible.

Phase 1

Foundation Build

Weeks 1-4 — Learn both disciplines. Kettlebell fundamentals + boxing basics. Build your base of strength, cardio, and technique.

Phase 2

Intensity Climb

Weeks 5-8 — Increase volume and complexity. Kettlebell complexes, boxing combination chains, hybrid conditioning sessions.

Phase 3

Peak Performance

Weeks 9-12 — Advanced programming: heavy kettlebell strength, fight-pace boxing, combined finishers. Test your limits.
