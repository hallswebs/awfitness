PROGRAMME OVERVIEW

Your 8-Week Journey

This programme is designed in 3 progressive phases. Each phase builds on the last, so you develop rock-solid technique before adding intensity. By week 8, you'll be swinging, pressing, and squatting with total confidence.

Phase 1

Learn & Move

Weeks 1-3 — Master the hip hinge, deadlift, goblet squat, and two-hand swing. Focus on mobility and movement quality.

Phase 2

Build & Strengthen

Weeks 4-6 — Introduce clean, press, and single-arm swings. Increase volume and add timed circuits for conditioning.

Phase 3

Perform & Progress

Weeks 7-8 — Full-body complexes, heavier loads, and conditioning finishers. Graduate ready for Kettlebell Power.
