THE SWEET SCIENCE

Why Boxing?

Boxing has been called "the sweet science" because it's the perfect blend of strategy, skill, and supreme physical conditioning. It's the oldest combat sport and arguably the most effective full-body workout ever devised.

At AW Fitness, you don't need to step into a ring. Alfie teaches boxing training for fitness, confidence, and self-discipline. You'll learn real technique from a professional fighter, combined with conditioning methods that will transform your body.

Whether you want to lose weight, build confidence, learn self-defence, or simply find a workout that makes you feel alive — boxing delivers on every level.

BENEFITS

What Boxing Does for Your Body & Mind

Boxing training delivers benefits that extend far beyond the gym. Here's what the science and experience tells us.

Supreme Fat Burning

Boxing training burns between 600-1,000 calories per hour. The combination of high-intensity striking, footwork drills, and conditioning rounds creates an afterburn effect that keeps your metabolism elevated for hours after training.

Elite Cardiovascular Fitness

Boxing rounds alternate between explosive bursts and active recovery, training both your aerobic and anaerobic systems. Research shows boxers have some of the highest VO2 max scores of any athletes.

Full-Body Strength

Every punch engages your legs, core, shoulders, and arms in a kinetic chain. Add in defensive movements, footwork, and conditioning, and you're building functional strength across your entire body.

Mental Health & Stress Relief

Studies show boxing significantly reduces anxiety and depression. The intense focus required during training clears your mind, while the physical exertion releases endorphins. Many clients say boxing is better than therapy.

Coordination & Reflexes

Boxing develops hand-eye coordination, reaction time, and spatial awareness that transfer to every sport and daily life. Your brain and body learn to work as one seamless unit.

Unshakeable Confidence

There's something transformative about learning to box. Knowing you can throw a proper combination and defend yourself builds a deep, quiet confidence that changes how you carry yourself in every area of life.

CURRICULUM

What You'll Learn

Alfie's boxing programmes follow a structured progression from complete beginner to advanced. Here's the journey.

Stance & Guard

Build a solid boxing foundation. Learn the proper fighting stance, guard position, weight distribution, and balance that everything else is built upon.

Basic Punches

Master the jab, cross, hook, and uppercut with perfect technique. Learn where power comes from (hint: it's not your arms) and how to throw each punch safely and effectively.

Footwork

Boxing footwork is an art. Learn how to move in all directions, pivot, cut angles, and control distance. Great footwork is what separates good boxers from great ones.

Combinations

String punches together into fluid combinations. From classic 1-2 jab-cross to complex 6-punch sequences with body shots, head movement, and level changes.

Defence

Learn slips, rolls, parries, and blocks. Great fighters are defined by their defence as much as their offence. You'll develop the ability to evade and counter effectively.

Conditioning

Fight-camp style conditioning rounds that build the gas tank of a professional boxer. Bag work, pad drills (for 1-to-1), shadow boxing circuits, and specialised boxing HIIT.

EQUIPMENT

What You'll Need

Boxing training at home requires minimal equipment. Here's what we recommend.

Boxing Gloves

12oz or 14oz gloves for bag work. Good gloves protect your hands and wrists and are essential for any bag work. Alfie recommends brands and sizes in the programme.

Hand Wraps

Essential for protecting your hands and wrists during bag work. Quick wraps or traditional wraps — video tutorials included in every programme.

Heavy Bag (Optional)

A heavy bag takes your training to another level, but it's not essential. Every session includes shadowboxing alternatives so you can train anywhere with zero equipment.
