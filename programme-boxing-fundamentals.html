PROGRAMME OVERVIEW

Your Boxing Journey Starts Here

Designed by an active professional boxer with 12 years of ring experience. You'll learn the same fundamentals that Alfie was taught — proper technique from day one, building into combinations and conditioning that'll transform your fitness.

Phase 1

Stance & Basics

Weeks 1-3 — Orthodox/southpaw stance, guard, jab, cross, basic footwork. Shadow boxing fundamentals and skipping conditioning.

Phase 2

Combinations

Weeks 4-6 — Hooks, uppercuts, 3-4 punch combinations, head movement, defensive footwork. Pad work patterns and bag rounds.

Phase 3

Ring Ready

Weeks 7-8 — Putting it all together. Timed rounds, combo drilling under fatigue, fight-style conditioning circuits.
